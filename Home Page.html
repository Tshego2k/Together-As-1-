TOGETHER AS 1 FOUNDATION

"Building a better tommorow one brick at a time"

Together As 1 is a construction organisation made up of a group of contracters/construction companies in the Northern region of South Africa with a one shared goal of creating a better community and overall society for disabled people. Our team is compiled of 12 esteemed construction companies with over 10 years experience in the construction market building unique and elegant homes for home owners,and are now doing the same for the diabled.We pride our selves in collaborating and doing things Together As 1 because we belive in the spirt of ubuntu . Together As 1s work is made possible through a 5% annual injection from all of our construction companies ,goverment assistance and donations in order for us to create personalized homes with great accessibility and easy-use overall functionality according to the needs of our clients.
